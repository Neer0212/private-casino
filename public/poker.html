Welcome to VIP Texas Hold'em

How the Game Works

  • The Ante: When a new hand begins, the server automatically pulls a ₹50 Ante from all seated players to start the Main Pot.
  • The Goal: Make the best 5-card poker hand using your 2 private "Hole" cards and the 5 public "Community" cards.
  • The Dealer Button: Since this is a custom home-game engine, anyone can click "Dealer: Advance Stage" to deal the next set of cards (Flop, Turn, River) once betting is done.

Betting Actions

  • Wait Your Turn: Your buttons will highlight green when it is your turn to act.
  • Check / Call: Match the current highest bet to stay in the hand. If no one has bet, this simply passes the turn (Check).
  • Raise: Increase the highest bet, forcing everyone else to pay more to stay in.
  • Fold: Throw away your cards. You forfeit any money you've put in the pot and sit out until the next hand.

The Showdown

  • After the River (the 5th community card), the math engine automatically flips everyone's cards and awards the entire Main Pot to the best hand!
